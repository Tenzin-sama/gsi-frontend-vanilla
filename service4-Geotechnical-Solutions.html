1. Natural Hazard Mitigation

GSI in collaboration with Trumer Schutznbauten GmbH, Austria are implementing rockfall, slope and debris flow protection measures in Nepal since 2014. Geohazard related any problems have been dealt with expert opinion and solutions are provided according to the site characteristics.

  1. Rockfall Protection Net:

    Rockfall protection systems by Trumer Schutzbauten GmbH consist of an interception structure and a support or retaining structure. The interception structure is formed by the Omega-Net with its outstanding deformability, flexibility and energy absorption. The support or retaining structure consists of posts, bearing ropes, retaining ropes and brake elements.

  2. Slope stabilization:

    The slope retention systems by Trumer Schutzbauten consist of the net or wire netting component, which constrains the blocks of rock or unstable material, and the anchors that channel the forces into the ground. The coupling of the netting to the anchors is achieved through either spike or clamp plates. As an option, support ropes can be attached to the netting and anchors in various configurations, distributing forces both vertically and horizontally.

  3. Avalanche Protection:

    The avalanche protection systems by Trumer Schutzbauten GmbH utilize the flexible nature and high strength of the Omega-Net to prevent avalanches in their first stage of occurrence. Additionally, in scenically sensitive areas, the nets are practically transparent and so are an aesthetic alternative to traditional snow bridge or snow rack systems.
